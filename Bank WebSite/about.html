About Us

Learn more about Greeny Credit Co. Op. Society Ltd.

Our Story

Established in 1995, Greeny Credit Co. Op. Society Ltd. has been serving the community for over 25 years. What started as a small cooperative society with just 50 members has now grown into a trusted financial institution with thousands of satisfied customers.

Our journey began with a simple mission - to provide accessible and affordable financial services to the local community. Over the years, we have expanded our services and reach while staying true to our core values of integrity, transparency, and customer-centricity.

Today, Greeny Credit Co. Op. Society Ltd. stands as a symbol of trust and reliability in the banking sector, offering a wide range of financial products and services designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our Mission

To empower our members and communities by providing accessible, affordable, and innovative financial solutions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and customer service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred financial institution, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and community development.
