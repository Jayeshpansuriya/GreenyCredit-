Our Deposit Products

Fixed Deposits

Our Fixed Deposit scheme offers you a secure way to invest your money for a fixed period at attractive interest rates. It's a perfect option for those looking for guaranteed returns on their investments.

Features & Benefits

  • Attractive interest rates
  • Flexible tenure options (1 month to 10 years)
  • Option for regular interest payouts
  • Loan facility available against FD
  • Auto-renewal facility

Monthly Recurring Deposits

Our Monthly Recurring Deposit scheme is designed to help you save regularly by depositing a fixed amount every month. It's an excellent way to build a substantial corpus over time through disciplined saving.

Features & Benefits

  • Attractive interest rates
  • Flexible tenure options (6 months to 10 years)
  • Minimum deposit amount: ₹500 per month
  • Loan facility available against RD
  • Auto-debit facility from savings account

Daily Recurring Deposits

Our Daily Recurring Deposit scheme is perfect for those who want to save on a daily basis. It's especially beneficial for small business owners, daily wage earners, and those who prefer to save small amounts regularly.

Features & Benefits

  • Attractive interest rates
  • Flexible tenure options (3 months to 5 years)
  • Minimum deposit amount: ₹50 per day
  • Option to deposit in advance for multiple days
  • Loan facility available against DRD
