Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan by visiting any of our branches, filling out the contact form on this page, or calling our loan department at +1 234 567 8900. Our loan officers will guide you through the application process and required documentation.

What documents are required for opening a deposit account?

To open a deposit account, you'll need to provide a valid ID proof (passport, driver's license, etc.), address proof, recent passport-sized photographs, and initial deposit amount. Additional documents may be required based on the type of account.

How can I check my account balance?

You can check your account balance through our mobile banking app, internet banking portal, by visiting any of our branches, or by calling our customer service helpline. We also offer SMS banking services for balance inquiries.

What is the interest rate for personal loans?

Our personal loan interest rates start from 10.5% per annum. The exact rate depends on your credit score, income, loan amount, and tenure. Please contact our loan department for a personalized quote.
